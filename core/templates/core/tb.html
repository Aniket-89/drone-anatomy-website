DA-20KGF Thrust Bench

DA-20KGF motor and propeller test stand is designed to test motors and propellers of medium UAV. It allows you to finish drone torque measuring of 20kg thrust for max 40'' propeller. The test stand will help you to figure out the most efficient drone propulsion system and extend the flight time.
The thrust tester is capable of testing the thrust, torque, voltage, current, RPM, motor and propeller efficiency of your drone propulsion system.
